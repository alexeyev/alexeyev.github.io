CONTROLS

  • D-pad — walk Sima around
  • A — talk / examine / advance
  • B — back / cancel
  • START — begin · advance
  • SELECT — open the Sketchbook
  • L — open the Konspekt

Tap the gear icon in the player to see or remap the keyboard bindings. On a phone, an on-screen gamepad appears automatically.

Runs in your browser via the mGBA core (WebAssembly), embedded with EmulatorJS.
