CONTROLS

  • D-pad — walk around (arrows on the keyboard)
  • A — talk / examine / advance (z on the keyboard)
  • B — back / cancel (x on the keyboard)
  • START — begin · advance (Enter on the keyboard)
  • SELECT — open the Sketchbook (v on the keyboard)
  • L — open the Konspekt (q on the keyboard)
  • R — e on the keyboard

Tap the gear icon in the player to see or remap the keyboard bindings. On a phone, an on-screen gamepad appears below the picture, handheld-style. Your konspekt, sketches, etc. are saved in your browser between visits.
