My name is Anton Alekseev (pronounced: [ɐlʲɪkˈsʲe(j)ɪf]), and I live and work in St. Petersburg, Russia. My pronouns are "he/him".

My main research interests lie in the field of ML-powered natural language processing.

I write papers and code; please see my CV for a more detailed tour of my career.
